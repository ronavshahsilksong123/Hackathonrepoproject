📖 HOW TO PLAY

🎯 Objective

Survive endless waves of enemies using your elemental magic. Each enemy you kill drops gold. Survive as long as possible and rack up the highest score!

🕹️ Controls

W / ↑ / Space — Jump
A / ← — Move Left
D / → — Move Right
Click — Cast current spell
1234 — Switch Spell
ESC / P — Pause
R — Restart (when dead)

✨ Spells

🔥 Fireball (1)
Creates fire explosions. Ignites oil & wood. Burns enemies over time. Cost: 8 mana.
❄️ Ice Bolt (2)
Freezes water into ice. Slows enemies on hit. Creates ice bridges. Cost: 5 mana.
⚡ Lightning (3)
Chains through water & sparks. High damage, wide spread. Cost: 15 mana.
🟢 Acid (4)
Dissolves terrain & enemies. Persists in the world. Very dangerous! Cost: 10 mana.

⚗️ World Physics

  • Sand falls and piles up realistically
  • Water and oil flow and spread sideways
  • Fire spreads to oil and wood — be careful!
  • Lava flows and ignites flammable materials
  • Acid dissolves most materials over time
  • Ice melts back into water slowly
  • Lightning chains through water — flood zones are deadly!

💀 Enemies

  • Red Crawler — basic melee enemy, chases you on the ground
  • Blue Jumper — fast, erratic jumping enemy
  • Orange Bomber — throws fire orbs at you from a distance
  • Enemies take damage from environmental hazards too — use the terrain!
  • Killing enemies drops gold (yellow pixels) — collect them!

💡 Tips

  • Use fire on oil pools for massive chain reactions
  • Flood areas then use lightning to hit multiple enemies at once
  • Acid can dig through stone if you aim carefully
  • Lava pools are deadly — don't fall in!
  • Enemies also take damage from fire and lava — lure them in!

ℹ️ ABOUT & CREDITS

🎮 About the Game

Elemental Chaos is a Noita-inspired pixel physics roguelite built entirely in HTML5 Canvas. Every pixel in the world obeys physical rules — sand falls, water flows, fire spreads, acid dissolves. You wield elemental magic to fight off waves of enemies in this fully destructible world.

🤖 How AI Was Used

Artificial Intelligence played a central role in creating this game:

  • Game Code: The entire game — physics engine, enemy AI, spell system, rendering pipeline, UI, and all screens — was designed and coded with the assistance of Claude (Anthropic), a large language model AI. Claude Sonnet 4.6 was used as the primary coding partner.
  • Enemy AI Behaviour: Enemy pathfinding and attack logic uses simple state machine AI designed with Claude's help.
  • Procedural World Generation: The terrain, platform placement, material distribution, and lava pool spawning is procedurally generated each run using algorithms developed with AI assistance.
  • Game Design Iteration: Claude was used to brainstorm spell mechanics, enemy types, and physics interactions during the design process.

👥 No Human Artists / External Assets

All visuals are rendered programmatically in HTML5 Canvas using JavaScript. No external images, sprites, sound files, or art assets were used. All fonts are loaded from Google Fonts (Press Start 2P, Share Tech Mono) — both are open source under the SIL Open Font License.
